EcoExplore Uganda – Site Plan

Site Name

EcoExplore Uganda – Chosen to reflect the eco-tourism focus and celebrate Uganda's natural beauty. This name combines "Eco" (representing environmental consciousness and ecotourism) with "Explore" (inviting adventure and discovery) and "Uganda" (showcasing our geographical focus). The name is memorable, descriptive, and clearly communicates our mission to promote sustainable tourism in Uganda.

Site Purpose

To promote sustainable travel experiences in Uganda, educate visitors about biodiversity, and support local communities through eco-friendly tours. The site will provide comprehensive information about Uganda's natural attractions, including national parks, forests, and wildlife. Visitors will be able to browse tour packages, learn about conservation efforts, read reviews from previous travelers, and contact us for bookings and inquiries.

Scenarios

  • What eco-tour options are available near the Source of the Nile?
  • How can I book a guided walk through Mabira Forest?
  • What wildlife can I see during an eco-tour in Uganda?
  • How does EcoExplore Uganda support local communities?

Color Scheme

The following colors have been carefully selected to evoke nature, sustainability, and the natural beauty of Uganda:

Forest Green

#228B22

Usage: Primary color for headers (h1, h2), navigation buttons, call-to-action buttons, and links. This color evokes nature, growth, and environmental consciousness.

Earth Brown

#8B4513

Usage: Secondary color for accents, borders, subheadings (h3), and footer backgrounds. This color reflects soil, wood, and the earthy tones of Uganda's landscapes.

Light Sage

#F5F9F6

Usage: Background color for the page body, providing a soft, natural backdrop that doesn't strain the eyes.

Charcoal

#2C2C2C

Usage: Body text and paragraph content for optimal readability and contrast.

Typography

Montserrat

Usage: Used for all headings (h1, h2, h3) to add modern flair and strong visual hierarchy. Weights: 400 (regular) and 700 (bold).

Sample Heading: Welcome to EcoExplore Uganda

Roboto

Usage: Used for body text, paragraphs, lists, and navigation links for excellent readability across all devices. Weights: 300 (light), 400 (regular), and 500 (medium).

Sample body text: Discover the natural wonders of Uganda through sustainable and responsible tourism. Our eco-tours support local communities while preserving the environment for future generations.
